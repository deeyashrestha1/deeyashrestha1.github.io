New Creation Pre School's Facilities

Our Facilities

Indoor & Outdoor Activities

We encourage students to engage in practical and hands on learning activities. These Activities are conducted inside as well as outside the classroom when necessary, under the supervision of learned tutors.

Celebrations & Programs

We not only promote academic growth but also foster the creativity that every child has inside them. We try to bring out the best in a child and make them a well rounded individual who is skilled in many different ways.

Extra Curricular Activities

Our aim is to create an environment for the children where they can freely express themselves. We do this by celebrating various festivals, occasions such as birthdays, and truly give a child a homelike experience.

Transportation & Day Care

Here at New Creation Pre-School We provide a transportation facilities that is punctual and reliable. We also provide scheduled meals and snacks to all students with a wide variety of nutritious and delicious food.
